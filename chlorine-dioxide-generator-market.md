In contrast, the chemical method generates chlorine dioxide by mixing sodium chlorite with acids, ensuring high production rates and effectiveness in water treatment applications. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and safety in chlorine dioxide generation.</p></p>
