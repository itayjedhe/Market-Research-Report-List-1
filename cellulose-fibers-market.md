<p><p>The cellulose fibers market is primarily divided into two types: natural cellulose fibers and man-made cellulose fibers. Natural cellulose fibers, derived from plants such as cotton, flax, and hemp, are valued for their biodegradability and breathability. Man-made cellulose fibers, produced from regenerated cellulose sources like wood pulp, include materials such as viscose, modal, and lyocell.
