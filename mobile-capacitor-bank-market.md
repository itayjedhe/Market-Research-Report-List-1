<p><p>The mobile capacitor bank market is categorized into two main types: single step and multi step. Single step capacitor banks provide a fixed level of reactive power compensation, making them simpler and more cost-effective for specific applications. In contrast, multi step capacitor banks offer adjustable compensation by incorporating multiple capacity levels, allowing for greater flexibility in responding to varying load demands. This versatility makes multi step solutions suitable for dynamic environments where power quality adjustments are frequently needed.</p></p>
