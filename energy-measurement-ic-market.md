<p><p>The energy measurement IC market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types based on functionality. Single-channel ICs measure energy consumption from one electrical load, making them ideal for simple applications like residential energy meters. In contrast, multi-channel ICs can monitor multiple loads simultaneously, suitable for complex systems such as industrial applications and smart grids. Multi-channel solutions offer enhanced data aggregation and analysis capabilities, enabling more efficient energy management and optimization across multiple devices or equipment.</p></p>
