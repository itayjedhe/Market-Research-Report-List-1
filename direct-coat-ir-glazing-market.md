<p><ul><li>Monolayer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct-Coat IR Glazing market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer coatings. Monolayer coatings consist of a single layer that effectively enhances infrared reflectivity while maintaining visible light clarity. In contrast, multilayer coatings involve multiple layers of materials, which work together to provide superior thermal insulation and improved energy efficiency. Both types are used in various applications, including residential and commercial buildings, to optimize energy performance and comfort by reducing heat loss or gain.</p></p>
