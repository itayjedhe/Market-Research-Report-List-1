<p><p>The commercial convection oven market includes two main types: countertop and floor model convection ovens. Countertop convection ovens are compact, designed for limited spaces, ideal for smaller establishments like cafés or food trucks, providing efficiency without requiring significant floor space. In contrast, floor model convection ovens are larger, built for high-volume operations such as restaurants and catering services, offering greater capacity and advanced features to accommodate extensive cooking needs. Both types enhance cooking speed and uniformity, catering to diverse culinary demands.</p></p>
