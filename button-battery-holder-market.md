<p><p>The button battery holder market can be categorized into three main types: surface mount, through hole, and others. Surface mount battery holders are designed for direct mounting onto the surface of a circuit board, ensuring compactness and ease of installation. Through hole battery holders, on the other hand, require holes to be drilled in the PCB, offering robust connections and reliability. The "others" category encompasses specialty designs, such as custom or unique mounting solutions tailored for specific applications, enhancing versatility in various devices.</p></p>
