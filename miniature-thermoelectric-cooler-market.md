<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Miniature Thermoelectric Cooler (TEC) market is segmented into single-stage and multi-stage types. Single-stage coolers operate by transferring heat across a single junction, making them compact and efficient for small applications. In contrast, multi-stage coolers consist of multiple thermoelectric junctions stacked together, allowing for greater temperature differentials and higher cooling performance for demanding applications like laser cooling and sensitive electronic components. Each type serves distinct requirements based on cooling capacity and thermal management needs.</p></p>
