<p><p>The Platinum Thermocouple Wire market comprises three main types: Type S, Type R, and Type B. Type S thermocouples, made from platinum-rhodium, are known for their high accuracy and stability, commonly used in laboratories. Type R, also platinum-rhodium, offers similar precision but operates at higher temperatures, making it suitable for industrial applications. Type B thermocouples, consisting of more rhodium, excel in extreme temperatures, often used in aerospace and high-temperature furnaces. Each type serves specific industry needs based on temperature range and accuracy.</p></p>
