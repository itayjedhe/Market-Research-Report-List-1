<p><p>The ferro manganese market is categorized into three main types based on carbon content: High Carbon, Medium Carbon, and Low Carbon. High Carbon Ferro Manganese contains around 70% manganese and 6-8% carbon, primarily used in steel production for deoxidation and alloying. Medium Carbon Ferro Manganese has a manganese content of about 70% and lower carbon levels (1-6%), providing better control over steel properties. Low Carbon Ferro Manganese, with less than 1% carbon, is ideal for producing high-quality steel with minimal impurities. Each type serves distinct applications in metallurgical processes.</p></p>
