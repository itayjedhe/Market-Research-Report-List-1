<p><p>The air conditioner cleaner market comprises two main types: spray and smear cleaners. Spray type cleaners are aerosolized solutions designed for easy application, allowing users to quickly coat the coils and surfaces, effectively dissolving dirt and grime. In contrast, smear type cleaners have a gel-like consistency that requires manual application, providing a more targeted approach to cleaning. Both types aim to enhance the efficiency and longevity of air conditioning systems by ensuring optimal airflow and heat exchange.</p></p>
