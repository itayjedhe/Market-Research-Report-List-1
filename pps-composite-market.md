<p><strong>The PPS Composite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-linked Type PPS Composite</li><li>Linear Type PPS Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS composites are specialized materials combining polyphenylene sulfide (PPS) with various fillers or reinforcements to enhance properties. The cross-linked type PPS composite features a networked molecular structure, providing superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Conversely, linear type PPS composite has a linear molecular structure, offering flexibility and ease of processing while still maintaining favorable chemical resistance and dimensional stability. Both types cater to different industrial needs based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
