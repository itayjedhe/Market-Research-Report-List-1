<p><ul><li>Contact Type Static Eliminators</li><li>Induction Type Static Eliminators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static eliminators market is primarily categorized into two types: contact type and induction type. Contact type static eliminators utilize direct contact with the surface to neutralize charges, providing effective charge removal in localized areas. In contrast, induction type static eliminators operate without direct contact, using electric fields to eliminate static charges from a distance, making them suitable for larger areas or delicate materials. Both types enhance product safety and quality across various industries by minimizing static-related issues.</p></p>
