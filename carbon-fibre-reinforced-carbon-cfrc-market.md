<p><strong>The Carbon Fibre Reinforced Carbon (CFRC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Fibre Reinforced Carbon (CFRC) is a composite material known for its high strength-to-weight ratio, thermal resistance, and durability. The market for CFRC can be categorized into two main types of manufacturing processes: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process (LIP). CVD involves the deposition of carbon fibers onto a substrate through gas reactions, yielding a uniform structure suited for high-performance applications. In contrast, the LIP method entails impregnating carbon fibers with a resin, offering cost-effective solutions suitable for various industries.</p></p>
