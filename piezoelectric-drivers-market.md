<p><p>The piezoelectric drivers market application encompasses both open loop and closed loop control systems. Open loop control lacks feedback, relying on predefined input signals to actuate piezoelectric devices, making it simpler and cost-effective for non-critical applications. Both systems leverage the unique properties of piezoelectric materials for applications in sensors, actuators, and precision positioning systems.</p></p>
