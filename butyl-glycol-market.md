<p><p>The Butyl Glycol market is typically categorized into two main types: E Series and P Series. The E Series, also known as ethylene glycol butyl ether, is primarily used in coatings, cleaning agents, and inks due to its excellent solvent properties. The P Series, or propylene glycol butyl ether, is favored for its lower toxicity and is commonly utilized in industrial applications, particularly in paints and personal care products. Both series cater to diverse industries, offering distinct advantages based on their chemical properties.</p></p>
