<p><p>The Knife Edge Gate Valve market can be categorized into bi-directional and unidirectional types. Bi-directional knife edge gate valves allow fluid flow in both directions, making them suitable for applications where flow direction changes frequently. Unidirectional knife edge gate valves, on the other hand, are designed to permit flow in a single direction, providing better control and preventing backflow.
