<p><strong>The UV Curable Resin For Optical Discs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radical UV-curable Resin</li><li>Cationic UV-curable Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Curable Resin market for optical discs features two primary types: radical UV-curable resin and cationic UV-curable resin. Radical UV-curable resins polymerize through a free radical mechanism, offering fast curing times and versatility in applications, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, cationic UV-curable resins use a different curing process involving cationic photoinitiators, providing superior thermal stability and chemical resistance. Both types are essential for enhancing the durability and performance of optical discs in various environments.</p></p>
