<p><p>The commercial bakery ovens market primarily includes convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens use a fan to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and efficient energy use, making them ideal for high-volume operations. Deck ovens, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers or "decks" where items are placed directly on heated stone or steel surfaces, providing superior crust quality and consistent results for artisanal products. Each type serves distinct baking needs, influencing performance and product quality.</p></p>
