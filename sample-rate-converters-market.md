<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Type</li><li>Synchronous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sample rate converters market is categorized into two main types: asynchronous and synchronous converters. Asynchronous converters transform audio signals between differing sample rates without a shared clock, accommodating independent systems. They are often used in digital audio workstations and streaming applications. In contrast, synchronous converters operate with a common clock, providing higher accuracy and lower jitter, making them ideal for live sound and professional audio setups. Each type caters to specific needs based on system integration and fidelity requirements.</p></p>
